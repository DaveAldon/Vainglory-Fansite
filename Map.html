THE HALCYON FOLD

  1. The Vain Crystal: Each team controls one and must protect it from the enemy. The goal of Vainglory is to destroy your opponent's Vain Crystal!
  2. Turrets: In order to destroy your opponent's Vain Crystal, you must destroy all of their turrets first. Minions run along the top lane and attack each other and the turrets, and it's up to you to help push them along and fight other heroes. Turrets are strong so be prepared! You could get ambushed easily from the bushes near each turret. Each Vain crystal is protected by 2 turrets as well, but once they go down anyone can attack your base!
  3. Minion Mines: Defeat the miners to gain control of each Minion Mine. Each mine under your team's control increases the power of your lane minion substantially, helping them destroy turrets and your opponent's Vain crystal!
  4. Shops: Each base has a shop to upgrade/buy items for your player. There's also one in the jungle to use, but be careful, you could easily be ambushed if you don't pay attention!
  5. Gold Miner/Kraken: Defeat the Gold Miner to gain a large gold bonus for your team (currency used to buy items), and after 15 minutes, the Kraken takes the miner's place. Attack the Kraken if you dare, and whatever team deals the final blow to her will gain her support and she'll fight for you. Controlling the Kraken often decides the winner of many games, she's strong so don't fight her alone!
