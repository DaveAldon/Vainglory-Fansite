Heroes of Vainglory

Heroes are at the heart of Vainglory, and are controlled by players in 3 versus 3 battles. Fortress is one of these characters, a canine hero of the halcyon fold. He is a support character who has abilities that are enhanced when around other players. This guardian is a snarling, frightening sight in the jungle. He stalks prey, closes in with teammates, flanks targets and creates challenging, multi-front confrontations. Stay near your allies and bite and claw your way to kills!

Heroes have three abilities unique to one another, and each ability provides help to teammates, or yourself, in order to gain the advantage. When a hero dies, they respawn after a certain amound of time and the player can jump right back into battle!
